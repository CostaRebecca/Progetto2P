About me

Federica Spagnoli, is a designer born in Rome on 10 July 2000. Thanks to his studies he is able to create visual design projects that provide innovative communication responses, he is able to prefigure new visual and communicative scenarios through a systemic vision of design by working in trans-disciplinary working groups.

Education

  • 2014\2019 - Ripetta Art High School, Final Rating: 93/100
  • 2019\2023 - Graduate in Design at Istituto per le Industrie Artistiche, Roma. Final rating: 110 cum laude/110
  • 2022\2024 - Master's Degree in Communication Systemics for Design at ISIA Rome.

Work

EGO skin,product design

EGO skin is a product in the Hi-Tech beauty sphere with the intention of making the practice of body care easier and more versatile. Its uniqueness lies in the use of multiple technologies in a single tool, making it possible to have a real beauty kit with you at all times.

Multi - subjet campaign

Create the perception of the city as a product through the creation of a strong and differentiated identity. The objective of the communication will be achieved with an advertising campaign to be extended across various media and supports.

Ready to go

Homes, offices, public and private spaces are undergoing a process of hybridization that has led to new ways of living, working, and traveling. The strength of "Ready To Go" furniture is its ability to adapt quickly to the changing spaces and needs of modern living.

Each X,design system

Each x, wants to raise awareness of the issue of biodiversity and does so through a print campaign that aims to intrigue and engage the user. Each x, in addition to the print campaign, raises awareness through multisensory experiences.

Four,the game to socialize

Four is a board-based multiplayer game that encourages sociability, providing players with an innovative way to make new friends. It consists of four different games. Each of them is designed to explore four different aspects of sociability and the players themselves.
